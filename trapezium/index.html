How do I calculate the area of a trapezium?

To calculate the area of a trapezium you would take the average of the two bases and multiply it by the height

Area = middle base × height

To calculate the middle base, it's basically just the arithmetic mean of both bases

Middle base = ( Top Base + Bottom Base) ÷ 2

So, in other words, the formula would simply be:

( ( Top Base + Bottom Base) ÷ 2 ) × height
